PDX International Flight Data

iPhone web app: host this folder, open index.html in Safari, then Share → Add to Home Screen.

Inbound Flight

Passengers, Bags, Assistance

Passenger Photo Capture reads the Acceptance Figures screen. It uses the AMS Total line in Total Acceptance Figures, Accepted C/W/M/Infant columns. You may also paste iPhone Live Text below, then tap Extract.
Wheelchair Photo Capture reads the Customer List services column. It counts rows containing WCHR into WCHR and rows containing WCHS into WCHC. You may also paste iPhone Live Text below, then tap Extract.

Connecting Flights

Photo Capture reads the Disruption Connection Summary screen. OCR works best when hosted on GitHub Pages/HTTPS. You may also paste iPhone Live Text below, then tap Extract.

Optional Outbound Data

Outbound Photo Capture reads the Acceptance Figures screen. It uses the PDX Total line in Total Acceptance Figures, Accepted C/W/M/Infant columns, then fills the Outbound Pax field. You may also paste iPhone Live Text below, then tap Extract.
